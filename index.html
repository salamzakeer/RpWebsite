About Parking Monitering System

Introduction

Security became a major concern these days in parking areas. Nowadays vehicles are rapidly increasing due to the rapid increase in parking traffic. Vehicle safe parking has become a serious problem for organizations. Some vehicles do not register legally or utilize the license plates of other vehicles. Those license plates can be used to determine the identification of a vehicle accused of committing a crime around the organizations. So only detecting the number plate as the vehicle detection for parking entrance is not safe.

For that proposing a novelty-based Smart Parking and Monitoring System to overcome this problem. Here, train the vehicle model using the neural network transfer learning technique to identify the vehicle model and classify the vehicles. The entrance of the organization detects and compares vehicle models with number plate details and operates the barrier system based on the vehicle’s authorization status.
